Roasted Brussel Sprouts:

1 lb brussel sprouts, trimmed and halved
½ tsp kosher salt
¼ tsp fresh ground pepper
1 Tbs extra virgin olive oil
½ tsp dried garlic powder

Combine ingredients in foil pan. Grill on high heat for 30-35 mins. Serve.
Apple Pie:

5 granny smith apples, peeled and sliced
1 red apple, peeled and sliced
1 ready-made pie crust
1/8 c flour
¼ c sugar
1/8 tsp fresh ground nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
¼ tsp salt

Place lower crust in 9 inch pie pan. Mix apples and other ingredients and place in lower crust. Place other crust on top, poke holes and seal at edges. Bake on 450 degrees at 10 minutes and then lower heat to 350 degrees and bake for another 50 minutes. Remove from oven to cool. Serve when ready, with vanilla ice cream.
Nana's Sauce

2 28 oz cans tomato sauce
1 tsp garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
3-4 lbs beef roast (any cut)
¼ tsp dried oregano, crushed
½ tsp dried basil
1 Tbs chopped fresh parsley

Place all ingredients in crock pot and cook on low 8+ hours. Serve over fresh pasta.
Chef's Salad

2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked
6 slices bacon, fried
1 avocado
½ cucumber, peeled and diced
½ ripe tomato, diced
2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
3 cups chopped romaine lettuce
2 Tbs vinaigrette dressing

Arrange lettuce on two plates. Create stripes of ingredients from left to right, starting with avocado, then tomato, cucumber, chicken, bacon, hard boiled egg and then drizzle with vinaigrette. Serve.
